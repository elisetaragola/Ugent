\section{The most beautiful equation}
Conversion of a complex number $z$ with $|z| = 1$ to it's complex form by using Euler's formula gives
\begin{equation}
z = \mathrm{e}^{i\phi} = \cos(\phi) + i\sin{\phi}.
\end{equation}
Set $\phi = \pi$, and thus
\begin{equation}
\mathrm{e}^{i\pi} = \cos(\pi) + i\sin{\pi} = -1.
\end{eqution}
Switch postions and we get the Most Beautiful Equation
$$\mathrm{e}^{i\pi}  + 1 = 0.$$
